Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup (75g) melted butter
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Butter or line a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until smooth.
  4. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  5. Mix in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  6. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and stir to combine.
  7. Add the flour and mix until just combined (don’t overmix).
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  9. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Let cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Slice and enjoy!
