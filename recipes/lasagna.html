Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 1 lb ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles (if not oven-ready):
    • Boil in salted water until al dente.
    • Drain and lay flat on parchment or a towel to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned.
    • Drain excess fat.
    • Add pasta sauce and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Make the cheese mixture:
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, 1/4 cup Parmesan, salt, and Italian seasoning.
  5. Assemble the lasagna (in a 9x13 inch baking dish):
    • Spread a bit of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Place lasagna noodles on top.
    • Spread ricotta cheese mixture.
    • Sprinkle mozzarella cheese.
    • Add meat sauce.
    • Repeat layers (usually 3 full sets).
    • Finish with noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and remaining Parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil (tent it so it doesn't touch the cheese).
  7. Bake for 25 minutes (covered).
  8. Remove foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
