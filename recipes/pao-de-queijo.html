Pao de Queijo

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (250g) tapioca flour
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (150g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, oil, and salt. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Remove from heat and slowly add the tapioca flour, stirring well until fully combined. The mixture will be thick and stretchy.
  4. Let the mixture cool for a few minutes (until warm but not hot to touch).
  5. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and beaten eggs. Mix until smooth. The dough will be sticky.
  6. Use a spoon or your hands to form small balls (about 1 to 1.5 inches wide).
  7. Place the dough balls on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving space between each.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until puffed and golden.
  9. Let cool slightly and enjoy warm!
